Growfax.eu Downloads

Windows
Steps for Windows:
1 - Press ⊞ Win+R (Windows Key + R) to open the "Run" window.
2 - Type C:\Windows\System32\drivers\etc in the textbox.
3 - Find file named hosts.
4 - Right-click on "hosts" and edit it with Notepad/text editor.
5 - Add the IP above.
6 - Then Save and log in to Growtopia.
iOS
Steps for iOS (Surge 5):
1 - Download App called "Surge 5" from App Store.
2 - Press "OK".
3 - Click "Default.conf".
4 - Click "IMPORT" → Download Profile from URL.
5 - Paste URL from the button above, click "OK" and "Done".
6 - Press "SETUP" and agree to the policy ("OK" → "Allow" for VPN Config).
7 - Done! Open Growtopia and connect.
PowerTunnel (Android)
Steps for Android:
1 - Download PowerTunnel App from Play Store.
2 - Click the "PowerTunnel" button above.
3 - It will copy the PowerTunnel host link.
4 - Open the PowerTunnel app, click the ☰ icon, then Host Settings.
5 - Paste the copied host URL. Change update period to "On Start".
6 - Now you can log in to your GTPS!
VirtualHost
Steps for VirtualHost:
1 - Download VirtualHost App (Android).
2 - Click "VirtualHost" button above to copy the host list.
3 - Open VirtualHost and paste the content into configuration.
4 - Activate VPN and enjoy Growfax GTPS!
